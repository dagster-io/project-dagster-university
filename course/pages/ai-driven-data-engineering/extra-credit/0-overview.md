# Extra credit: Behind the skills

You've finished the main course. If you want to understand how the Dagster skills are built and kept accurate as the product evolves, this section goes behind the scenes.

---

## What's here

**How we evaluate our skills** — The evaluation framework we use to ensure skills produce correct Dagster code, not just plausible-looking output. We cover what we validate, what we've learned from iterating on the skills, and how we feed those learnings back in.

This content is optional. It's aimed at engineers who are curious about the methodology, want to build and evaluate skills of their own, or just want a deeper understanding of what makes a skill reliable.
